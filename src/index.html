F.A.Q

To adopt a cat, visit our shelter during our opening hours. Our staff will guide you through the adoption process, help you meet our available cats, and provide information on the adoption fees and requirements.

The adoption fee covers essential services such as spaying/neutering, vaccinations, microchipping, and a basic health check. It ensures that your new feline friend is ready to start their journey in a loving home.

Absolutely! We encourage potential adopters to spend time interacting with our cats. This helps you understand their personalities and ensures a good match. Our staff will be happy to assist you in finding a cat that suits your lifestyle.

Yes, we provide post-adoption support and resources. Feel free to reach out to us if you have any questions or concerns about your newly adopted cat. We are here to offer advice, guidance, and assistance as you and your new companion adjust to each other.

If you find a lost or stray cat, please contact your local animal control or bring the cat to our shelter if possible. We can scan for a microchip, check for identification, and provide a safe and temporary home while attempting to locate the owner. Your efforts help reunite lost pets with their families.
